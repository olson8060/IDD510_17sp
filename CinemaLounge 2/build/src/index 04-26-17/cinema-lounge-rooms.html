About our rooms

Not your average "hangout and binge-watch" day.

Feel free to lounge in our heated leather seats with a blanket and your favorite cocktail. Plus, we’ve added features like pause and play, on-screen cast bios and show trivia, and your very own watchlist. Just start adding shows and movies online, and reserve a room today!
