New Technologies

We know YOU and what YOU love. Our state-of-the-art database allows us to customize your ticket purchasing and on-property ordering experience.

One of the best features about Cinema Lounge is our emphasis on using new technologies. Buy tickets easily through our website, send an auto-text to your friends when buying for a group, and order food and drinks from your in-seat kiosk using your login credentials and card pin. Not a member online? Have no fear. Simply swipe your credit or debit card in the in-seat kiosk and order as a guest.


Our Mission

To revitalize the movie-goers experience.

Cinema Lounge aims to bring both new technologies and old-world charm into one place. Considered to be the premeire venue for cocktails, dinner, and a movie, we take pride in our service, atmosphere, and style.


History

Delicious popcorn, classy atmosphere, and a spectacular movie variety has been the key to our success.

Since openning in 2015, Cinema Lounge has moved from the historic 1500 building in Philadelphia to three new locations across the country. In 2016, Cinema Lounge started to integrate new computer systems and started to offer in-seat kiosk ordering. After revamping our services and coaching our staff on how to better serve our guests we have reached a whole new level. We are truly making history.
